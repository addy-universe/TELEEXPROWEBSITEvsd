Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about TeleExPro (Teleexpro), our jobs, services, and how to connect with us.

What is TeleExPro (Teleexpro)?

TeleExPro — also commonly written as Teleexpro, teleexpro.in, or Tele Ex Pro — is a premier business solutions and recruitment company headquartered in Gurugram, Haryana, India. Founded to bridge the gap between talented professionals and growing international businesses, Teleexpro specializes in telecalling services, customer support outsourcing, lead generation, CRM management, and back-office operations from our state-of-the-art corporate office at Sector 36, Gurugram.

Where is the Teleexpro office located?

The TeleExPro (Teleexpro) corporate office is located at Plot No 2, Sector - 36, Narsinghpur, opposite Skyview Corporate Park, Gurugram, Haryana 122004, India. Our office is easily accessible from major Gurugram landmarks and is situated in a prime commercial zone near Sector 36 market. We operate Monday through Saturday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM.

Does Teleexpro offer jobs in Gurugram?

Yes! TeleExPro is actively hiring for multiple office-based positions at our Gurugram location. Current openings at Teleexpro include Telecalling & Sales Executive, Customer Support Representative, and Team Leader — Sales Operations. We welcome both freshers and experienced candidates. Salaries range from ₹10,000 to ₹35,000 per month plus unlimited performance incentives. Visit our Teleexpro Careers page to apply directly.

What business services does TeleExPro provide?

TeleExPro (Teleexpro) provides comprehensive business solutions including: Customer Support Outsourcing (24/7 omni-channel support via email, chat, and phone), Telecalling & Outbound Sales (cold calling, warm list dialing, inbound lead conversion), Lead Generation & Pipeline Building (B2B and B2C audience targeting, lead qualification, appointment scheduling), and Backend & CRM Administration (HubSpot, Salesforce, Zoho management, data entry, order tracking). All services are delivered from our professional Gurugram office. Visit our Services page for details.

How to contact Teleexpro?

You can reach TeleExPro (Teleexpro) through multiple channels: Email us at hr@teleexpro.in for job applications or business inquiries. Visit our official website at teleexpro.in. Or visit our corporate office in person at Plot No 2, Sector 36, Narsinghpur, Gurugram 122004. For career-related queries, you can also use our Employee Query Box on the careers page. Follow us on LinkedIn for the latest updates.

What is the salary at Teleexpro?

TeleExPro offers competitive, assured monthly salaries that are always credited on time. Salary ranges at Teleexpro depend on the role: Sales Executives earn ₹12,000 – ₹25,000/month, Customer Support Representatives earn ₹10,000 – ₹20,000/month, and Team Leaders earn ₹18,000 – ₹35,000/month. All positions include performance-based incentives and bonuses with no cap. Teleexpro also offers rapid career progression, professional mentoring, and a supportive team culture.
